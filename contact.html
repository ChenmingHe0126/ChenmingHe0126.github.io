Chenming He

"As an architect, I try to be guided not by habit but by a conscious sense of the past - by precedent, thoughtfully considered... As an artist, I frankly write about what I like in architecture: complexity and contradiction." Robert Venturi

Chasing Complexity has always been a consistent theme in my designs. In the real world, what attracts me most is the unimaginable diversity and adaptability caused by the complicated connection of various factors. I believe these natural essences should be discovered, cherished, developed, and displayed.

My work explores contradictions and complexity generated among architecture, cities, society, nature, human emotions, and art.
